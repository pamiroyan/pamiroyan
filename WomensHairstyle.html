Women's Hairstyle

Whether you want to get some new looks into rotation, or just want to try a different hairstyle for a special occasion, we have all the inspiration you need. With photo galleries, styling tips and easy-to-follow tutorials, you need never be stuck for hairstyle ideas again! Whatever your hair length and texture, you're bound to find a look you'll love.

All Style

Hair Bangs

The best and simplest way to breathe life into your hair? By getting some bangs cut in! It's suitable for any hair type, and you can adapt it to your face and style. Find out what kind of bangs will work best for your face shape and your existing hairstyle and then go for it! Once you know how, bangs are super easy to style and can give your look a gorgeous update without a radical all-over cut.

Braid hairstyles

This style is ideal for long or medium hair. It offers a million possibilities-- literally. There's the fishtail braid, the side braid, the french braid... the choice is yours!

Ponytail hairstyles

The ponytail hairstyle is timeless, and it's suitable for all hair types. Whether your hair is long or short, curly or straight, there's a ponytail out there to suit you! The variants are endless and the style can fit to any look you want!

Hair accessories

Headbands, clips, pins, flowers... there are all kinds of hair accessories out there that can add some pretty detail to your hairstyle and maybe even have a practical use, too! Find out how to style them with your favorite hairstyles, discover which textures work with which accessories and maybe even find a new look you haven't tried yet!

Layered haircuts

Don't chop, feather! Layered hairstles create a soft, romantic look and stop your hair from looking (and feeling) too heavy. They also create movement and bounce. If you've got a layered cut, then we know just how to style it!.

Bob haircuts

Long or short, straight or asymmetrical, the bob is always on trend. Check out the hairstyle that is both easy to style and actually pretty versatile.

Retro hairstyles

Retro hair is back... that is, if it ever went anywhere! Either way, we've got a whole lot of different throwback styles that are just as fashionable now as they were then. We'll guide you through beautiful vintage hairstyles from soft waves from the '50s to the '80s afro curly, focusing on which products to use to recreate your hairstyle.

Messy hairstyles

Do neat hairstyles seem too serious for you? Even good girls have fun with their hair! whether your hair is long, short, straight or curly, there is a messy style out there for you!

Ombre hair

Ombre hair is exploding on the hair scene right now. From the red carpet to the high street, it seems to be everywhere. This dip dye effect adds a touch of fun to your hair, and has an amazing illuminating effect. Let the light in!

Updo hairstyles

The bun is classic and chic hairdo that is perfect for those who have long or mid-length hair. From classy to messy, your bun can take on as many personalities as you can! Find out how to reinvent the traditional bun!

Wet look hair

The wet look is a catwalk staple, and we can see why. This look works for any hair type and it's sleek, smooth and sexy. Oh, and did we mention edgy?

Half Hawk hairstyles

The half hawk is the latest style in the world of hair styling. If you were brave enough to get a half hawk , we have the hairstyles that are best for you! Who says that you didn't go all the way?
